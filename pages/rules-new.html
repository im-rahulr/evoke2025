LOWRY ADVENTIST COLLEGE

Besides K R Pura Metro Station, Dooravaninagar, Bengaluru - 16

INTER-SCHOOL FEST

Evoke 2025 • “Bring out the best in You.”

RULES TO BE FOLLOWED

  • Registration for all the events starts from 8:00 AM to 8:30 AM.
  • Each participant must carry their school ID at all times.
  • Participants must be present for the inaugural program at 8:30 AM.
  • Teams that are late or miss their slot for any event will be disqualified.
  • Partaking in any kind of obscenity will lead to the debarring of the team from any further participation.
  • Illegal or restricted items such as alcohol, tobacco or drugs are strictly prohibited. Weapons, sharp objects or harmful items are not allowed.
  • Participants must strictly follow the code of conduct and maintain discipline.
  • Participants must carry the required equipment for their events.
  • The organizers reserve the right to cancel, reschedule the time or change the venue of any event. A minimum of three teams (for team event or 3 participants for individual events) must be there for an event or it will be cancelled.
  • Decision of the judges and coordinators will be final.
  • Tracks for solo dance, group dance, solo instrumental must be submitted in a pendrive at the time of registration.
  • The management will not be responsible for the loss of personal belongings.
  • A participant may register for a maximum of two events.
  • Lunch will be provided for all the Participants.
  • The final event of the day will conclude at 5:00pm.
  • The last date for the FEST registration is 20th November 2025.
  • Participants who register prior for the FEST shall pay a fee of ₹100 per Event.
  • Participants who register on the day of the FEST Shall pay a fee of ₹150 per Event.

EVENTS AND RULES

  • 1 Dumb Charades
    • A team must consist of 2 participants.
    • One participant acts out the word or phrase without speaking/sound/no lip-syncing.
    • Another participant must guess the word/phrase enacted.
    • Time limit allotted for guessing is 1 minute.
    • A buzzer will indicate the end of the allotted time for the round.
    • Each team will receive 10 words per round out of which a minimum of 8 must be answered correctly to qualify for the round.
    • Judges' decision is final.
  • 2 G.K. Quiz

    There will be two stages for this event

    • Prelims written test will be conducted.
    • Two members per team.
    • Maximum of two teams per school.
    • The quiz will be based on Aptitude, Current Affairs, Sports, Mathematics, Science, Inventions, and Geography.
    • 4 qualified teams in the prelims written test will be participating in the final oral round.
    • Judges' decision will be final.
  • 3 Fashion Show: Theme: Indian Culture

    Individual Participation

    • The participants or outfits must adhere to the given theme.
    • Clothing should be appropriate and modest. (Sleeveless attire, above the knee dresses, scandalous outfits, or ripped cloths are not allowed).
    • Props, hats, bags, accessories can be utilized.
    • The time limit will be 30 seconds on stage.
    • The decision of the judges will be final.
  • 4 Group Song
    • Each team may consist of 3 to 5 members.
    • Performance time should be maximum 4 minutes.
    • Songs may be in any language, but must be appropriate and decent.
    • Participants will be judged based on Vocal Performance, Musicality, Stage Presence, Technical Skill and Performance Style.
    • Participants can bring their own track/karaoke.
    • The decision of the judges will be final.
    • No religious Songs.
  • 5 Solo Song
    • Solo singing event.
    • The time limit will be 2 minutes maximum.
    • Songs must be decent and may be in any language, No religious songs.
    • Participant can bring own track/karaoke.
    • The decision of the judges will be final.
  • 6 Group Dance
    • Each team must consist of a minimum of 2 and maximum of 5 members.
    • Theme - Cinematic
    • The time limit will be 3+1 minutes, Fusion is allowed.
    • Judgement will be based on Choreography, Execution, Expression and Performance, Costume and Co-ordination, No Religious acts.
    • Participants must bring their own track in a pen drive.
    • Participants are prohibited from usage of any substances which makes the stage dirty.
    • Carrying obscene or vulgar music is strictly prohibited.
    • Maximum 2 teams can participate from each School.
    • The decision of the judges will be final.
  • 7 Solo Dance
    • Individual Participation.
    • Time limit is 4 minutes.
    • Theme - Free Style, No Religious acts.
    • Fusion is allowed.
    • Dancers have to bring their songs on a pen drive.
    • Participants must be decent and perform gracefully.
    • The decision of the judges will be final.
    • Participants are prohibited from usage of any substances which make the stage dirty.
  • 8 Instrumental Music
    • Bring your own instruments.
    • Time limit 3 minutes.
    • Individual performance only.
    • The judge's decision will be final.
  • 9 Face Painting
    • Maximum 2 participants for a team. (1 painting on the other face)
    • Participants must bring their own materials.
    • Time limit is 1 hour.
    • Participants will be judged on the basis of creativity, Artistic Composition, Originality and Clarity of Theme.
    • The decision of the judges will be final.
    • Free Theme - (Draw on any Theme)
  • 10 Cooking Without Fire
    • Individual Participant only, Time limit 1½ hour.
    • Participant must prepare dishes without using fire.
    • Participant are required to bring their own Utensils
    • Ready-to-cook or precooked items are not allowed.
    • Presentation and overall creativity will be judged. (Nutrition Value)
    • Only snacks, salads, desserts, or beverages are allowed.
    • Name of your dish and ingredients must be displayed.
    • The decision of the judges will be final.
  • 11 Vegetable Carving
    • Participant will be given 1 hour to complete their carving.
    • The participation is individual.
    • Carving must follow the given theme.
    • Participants must bring their own vegetables, tools, and accessories.
    • Use of fire, electric tools, or pre-cut items are not allowed.
    • The judge's decision will be final.
  • 12 Pot Painting
    • Pot will be provided.
    • Only one pot per participant is allowed.
    • Participants must carry their own painting materials – brushes, paint palette, etc.
    • Use of fire or spray paint is not allowed.
    • Time limit: 1 hour.
    • Only hand painting is allowed – no printed stickers, decals, or readymade decorations.
    • The pot must remain unbroken and cleanly finished.
    • The judge's decision will be final.
  • 13 Art and Craft
    • Only one participant is allowed.
    • Participant must bring their own materials and tools (papers, colors, brushes, glue, scissors, etc.).
    • Time duration is 1 hour.
    • Original work only – copying from the internet or books will lead to disqualification.
    • Use of mobile phones is not allowed.
    • The judge's decision will be final.
  • 14 Collage Competition
    • Minimum 2 members to form a team.
    • Participants must bring their own materials (magazines, newspapers, coloured papers, glue, etc.).
    • The theme will be announced on the spot.
    • Time duration will be 1 hour.
    • Charts will be provided.
    • Only handmade collages are allowed.
    • All work must be creative and original.
    • Using blades, scissors, fire and paints are strictly not allowed.
    • The judge's decision will be final.
  • 15 Pencil Sketch
    • Only one participant is allowed.
    • The theme will be announced on the spot.
    • Participant must bring their own drawing materials – pencil, erasers and sharpeners.
    • Papers will be provided.
    • Only graphite pencils or charcoal pencils are allowed.
    • Time duration will be 1 hour.
    • No obscene sketching should be done.
    • The sketch must be original and creative according to the theme.
    • Judges' decision will be final.
  • 16 Best Out of Waste
    • Individual Participants.
    • The time limit will be 1½ hour.
    • Participants must bring their own waste materials and tools (scissors, glue, tape, etc.).
    • Only waste or recyclable materials (plastic bottles, newspapers, cardboard, old CDs, etc.) should be used.
    • Participants must create their model on the spot — no pre-prepared items are allowed.
    • The item should be useful, creative, and environmentally friendly.
    • Electric or battery-operated tools are not allowed.
    • Judges' decision will be final.
