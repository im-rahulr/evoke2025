On-Stage and Off- Stage Events Rule book










 
RULES TO BE FOLLOWED

1. Registration for all the events starts from 8:00 AM to 8:30 AM.

2. Each participant must carry their school ID at all times.

3. Participants must be present for the inaugural program at 8:30 AM.

4. Teams that are late or miss their slot for any event will be disqualified.

5. Partaking in any kind of obscenity will lead to the debarring of the team from any further participation.

6. Illegal or restricted items such as alcohol, tobacco or drugs are strictly prohibited. Weapons, sharp objects or harmful items are not allowed.

7. Participants must strictly follow the code of conduct and maintain discipline.

8. Participants must carry the required equipment for their events.

9. The organizers reserve the right to cancel, reschedule the time or change the venue of any event. A minimum of three teams must be there for an event or it will be canceled.

10. Decision of the judges & coordinators will be final.

11. Tracks for solo dance, group dance, solo instrumental must be submitted in a pendrive at the time of registration.

12. The management will not be responsible for the loss of personal belongings.

13. A participant may register for a maximum of two events.

14. Lunch will be provided for all the Participants.

15. The final event of the day will conclude at 5:00pm. 

16. The last date for the FEST registration is 20th November 2025.

17. Participants who register prior for the FEST shall pay a fee of ₹100 per Event .

18. Participants who register on the day of the FEST Shall pay a fee of ₹150 per Event.    	   






1 Dumb Charades:
1.	A team must consist of 2 participants.
2.	One participant acts out the word or phrase without speaking/sound/no lip-syncing.
3.	Another participant must guess the word/phrase enacted.
4.	Time limit allotted for guessing is 1 minute.
5.	A buzzer will indicate the end of the allotted time for the round.
6.	Each team will receive 10 words per round out of which a minimum of 8 must be answered correctly to qualify for the round.
7.	Judges’ decision is final.

2 G.K. Quiz:
1.	Prelims written test will be conducted.
2.	Two members per team.
3.	Maximum of two teams per school.
4.	The quiz will be based on Aptitude, Current Affairs, Sports, Mathematics, Science, Inventions, and Geography.
5.	4  qualified teams in the prelims written test will be participating in the final oral round.
6.	Judges’ decision will be final.

3 Fashion Show: Theme: Indian Culture
Individual Participation
1.	The participants or outfits must adhere to the given theme.
2.	Clothing should be appropriate and modest.(Sleeveless attire,below the knee dresses, scandalous outfits,or ripped cloths are not allowed.
3.	Props, hats, bags, accessories can be utilized.
4.	The time limit will be 30 seconds on stage.
5.	The decision of the judges will be final.


4 Group Song:
1.	Each team may consist of 3 to 5 members.
2.	Performance time should be maximum 4 minutes.
3.	Songs may be in any language, but must be appropriate and decent.
4.	Participants will be judged based on Vocal Performance, Musicality,Stage Presence, Technical Skill and Performance Style.
5.	Participants can bring their own track/karaoke.
6.	The decision of the judges will be final.
7.	 No religious Songs.


5 Solo Song:
1. Solo singing event.
2. The time limit will be 2 minutes maximum.
3. Songs must be decent and may be in any language, No religious songs.
4. Participant can bring own track/karaoke.
5. The decision of the judges will be final. 


6 Group Dance:
1. Each team must consist of a minimum of 2 and maximum of 5 members.
2. Theme - Cinematic
3. The time limit will be 3+1 minutes, Fusion is allowed.
4. Judgement will be based on Choreography,Execution, Expression and Performance, Costume and Co-ordination, No Religious acts.
5. Participants must bring their own track in a pen drive.
6. Participants are prohibited from usage of any particles which makes the stage dirty.
7. Carrying obscene or vulgar music is strictly prohibited.
8. Maximum 2 teams can participate from each School.
9. The decision of the judges will be final.

7 Solo Dance:
1. Individual Participation .
2. Time limit is 4 minutes.
3. Theme - Free Style, No Religious acts.
4. Fusion is allowed.
5. Dancers have to bring their songs on a pen drive.
6. Participants must be decent and perform gracefully.
7. The decision of the judges will be final.
8. Participants are prohibited from usage of any particle which make the stage dirty.

8  Instrumental Music:
1. Bring your own instruments.
2. Time limit 3 minutes.
3. Individual performance only.
4. The judge’s decision will be final.

9  Face Painting:
1. Maximum 2 participants for a team.(1 painting on the other face)
2. Participants must bring their own materials.
3. Time limit is 1 hour.
4. Participants will be judged on the basis of creativity, Artistic Composition, Originality and Clarity of Theme..
5. The decision of the judges will be final.
6. Free Theme - (Draw on any Theme)

10 Cooking Without Fire:
1. Individual Participant only, Time limit  1½ hour.
2. Participant must prepare dishes without using fire.
3. Participant are required to bring their own Utensils 
4. Ready-to-cook or precooked items are not allowed.
5. Presentation and overall creativity will be judged.(Nutrition Value)
6. Only snacks, salads, desserts, or beverages are allowed.
7. Name of your dish and ingredients must be displayed.
8. The decision of the judges will be final.



11 Vegetable Carving:    
1.	Participant will be given 1 hour to complete their carving.
2.	The participation is individual.
3.	Carving must follow the given theme.
4.	Participants must bring their own vegetables, tools, and accessories.
5.	Use of fire, electric tools, or pre-cut items are not allowed.
6.	The judge’s decision will be final.


12 Pot Painting
1.	Pot will be provided.
2.	Only one pot per participant is allowed.
3.	Participants must carry their own painting materials – brushes, paint palette, etc.
4.	Use of fire or spray paint is not allowed.
5.	Time limit: 1 hour.
6.	Only hand painting is allowed – no printed stickers, decals, or readymade decorations.
7.	The pot must remain unbroken and cleanly finished.
8.	The judge’s decision will be final.


13 Art and Craft
1.	Only one participant is allowed.
2.	Participant must bring their own materials and tools (papers, colors, brushes, glue, scissors, etc.).
3.	Time duration is 1 hour.
4.	Original work only – copying from the internet or books will lead to disqualification.
5.	Use of mobile phones is not allowed.
6.	The judge’s decision will be final.



14 Collage Competition
1. Minimum 2 members to form a team.
2. Participants must bring their own materials (magazines, newspapers, coloured papers, glue, etc.).
3. The theme will be announced on the spot.
4. Time duration will be 1 hour.
5. Charts will be provided.
6. Only handmade collages are allowed.
7. All work must be creative and original.
8. Using blades,scissors,fire and paints are strictly not allowed.
9. The judge’s decision will be final. 

15 Pencil Sketch
1.	Only one participant is allowed.
2.	The theme will be announced on the spot.
3.	Participant must bring their own drawing materials – pencil, erasers and sharpeners. 
4.	Papers will be provided.
5.	Only graphite pencils or charcoal pencils are allowed.
6.	Time duration will be 1 hour.
7.	No obscene sketching should be done.
8.	The sketch must be original and creative according to the theme.
9.	Judges’ decision will be final.


16 Best Out of Waste
1.	Individual Participants.
2.	The time limit will be 1½ hour.
3.	Participants must bring their own waste materials and tools (scissors, glue, tape, etc.).
4.	Only waste or recyclable materials (plastic bottles, newspapers, cardboard, old CDs, etc.) should be used.
5.	Participants must create their model on the spot — no pre-prepared items are allowed.
6.	The item should be useful, creative, and environmentally friendly.
7.	Electric or battery-operated tools are not allowed.
8.	Judges’ decision will be final.
